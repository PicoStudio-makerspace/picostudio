Who We Are

Built by makers,
for makers.

Pico Studio is a not-for-profit incorporated association based in Coburg North. We exist to make creative and maker work accessible — by sharing the cost of space, tools, and equipment across a community of people who love building things.

🤝
Community-run
Governed by our members. Every decision is made in the interest of the community.
💸
Affordable access
Shared costs mean more people can access quality tools and space.
🌱
Not-for-profit
Any surplus goes back into improving the space and supporting members.

The Workshop

Shared tools,
serious capability.

Our shared workshop is stocked with quality tools and equipment. Members get access to everything they need — without the cost of owning it all yourself.

  • Woodworking tools & benches
  • Metalworking & fabrication
  • Hand tools & power tools
  • Safety gear & inductions provided
  • Growing equipment library
